Welcome to “SMVDU Online Grievance Redressal Portal”. This portal has been created to provide convenience to students, staff and faculty by allowing them to lodge a grievance through their mobile phones or computers. This removes the need for them to appear at any location to file a physical grievance. Now filing of grievance as well as tracking the status of the same is possible online through this portal. This saves a large amount of time and effort.

welcome

Welcome to the Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University Online Grievance Monitoring System. This grievance is channelized into one cohesive system with a Unique Grievance Tracking Number.

Some of the advantage of this system are below:-


Easy to use
  • Allow registration of grievance through internet 24 x 7.
  • No physical actions required.
quick feedback
  • Unique registration number to the students upon submission of the grievance to the system.
  • Any Question or documents required for redress of the grievance will be asked in one go on a time bound basis on receipt of the grievance.
transparency
  • Grievances cannot be deleted by anyone and most of the information is available with the students.
  • Facility to submit reminder or clarification related to the grievance.
